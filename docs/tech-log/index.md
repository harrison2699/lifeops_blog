# Tech Logs

Weekly, timestamped notes that capture **what changed**, **what broke**, and **what stuck**. Logs stay short; deeper material graduates to **[Articles](/articles/)** when it is worth the extra polish.

## Why weekly

Robotics weeks are dense. A lightweight rhythm keeps continuity without pretending every day needs a headline. When a month ends, I skim logs and promote one or two threads into public articles.
