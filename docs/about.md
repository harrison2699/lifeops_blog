# About

I am a **robotics software engineer** focused on reliable field software: **ROS 2**-based architectures, **robot control** and **manipulation**, and **Physical AI** systems where perception, planning, and actuation meet messy reality.

## How I work

I care about systems that stay legible after handoff—clear interfaces, observable behavior, and tests where they buy the most confidence. I enjoy **technical writing** when it sharpens thinking: a good note today saves days of re-discovery later.

## Interests

- **ROS 2** integration patterns, performance, and operational concerns on real hardware  
- **Control and manipulation** for contact-rich tasks and human-scale machines  
- **Physical AI** stacks: data, simulation, evaluation, and honest sim-to-real gaps  
- **Long-term learning**: revisiting fundamentals, reading primary sources, and keeping a steady writing habit alongside deep implementation work  

## What you will not find here

This site is intentionally public. It avoids sensitive **company internal** information, unreleased product specifics, customer-identifying details, and anything that would not be appropriate outside a private lab notebook.
